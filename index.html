My father, whose love knows no bounds.

"The journey of my life with my father"

  • 1999 - A Blessed Beginning
    I was born, and for the first time, my father held me in his arms. It was a lucky day for him, but even luckier for me to have a father like him.
  • 2004- First Day of School
    At the age of five, I was admitted to school. My father held my hand and walked me to class, his presence filling me with confidence and comfort.
  • 2010 - Proud Moment at Primary School
    I took my Primary School Certificate (PSC) exam and achieved first division! My father was so proud; he brought sweets home to celebrate our joy.
  • 2013- Junior School Success
    With my father’s constant encouragement, I completed my Junior School Certificate (JSC) exam, grateful for his support every step of the way.
  • 2016- Milestone in High School
    I passed my Secondary School Certificate (SSC) exam. My father was there, cheering me on, reminding me of his faith in my abilities.
  • 2018- High School Graduation
    Completing my Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) exam was a proud moment for both of us. My father celebrated my achievements as if they were his own.
  • 2019 - Entering University
    My first day in my Bachelor of Science program in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). My father’s excitement was boundless, and he reassured me of his belief in my dreams.
  • 2021- A Test of Faith During COVID-19
    The world faced the COVID-19 pandemic, and my father fell seriously ill. He was hospitalized, and our hearts were weighed down with worry. By the grace of Almighty Allah, he recovered, a blessing that filled us all with immense gratitude.
  • 2023- Completing My Bachelor's Degree
    I graduated with my Bachelor’s degree in CSE, and my heart was full of new dreams. I told my father I wanted to take a different path in life, something unique. He listened, smiled, and gave me his full support, as he always has.
  • Present- A Father’s Endless Care
    Even today, he makes me tea and often asks me to work alongside him. My father, my hero, fills my days with warmth and joy, always believing in my potential and pushing me to be my best.
"In his care, I see Allah's mercy, in his love, I feel Allah's blessings. A father’s dua is a shield, protecting me in ways unseen. His sacrifices reflect the Sunnah of patience and dedication. Through his guidance, I walk the path closer to Allah. Truly, paradise lies in serving and honoring him."
